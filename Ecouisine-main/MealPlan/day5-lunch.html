Southwest Rotisserie Chicken Salad

Throw the rest of your rotisserie chicken in this colorful lunch salad that will satisfy even the heartiest of appetites. Packed with budget-friendly but healthy ingredients, this is a lunch that will leave you feeling energized - no post-lunch slump for you!
Click to Start

Prep

01.

Rotisserie chicken | If not already done, tear meat off of the bone and chop into bite-sized pieces. (Can be done up to 3 days ahead)

02.

Romaine / Red peppers / Corn / Black beans | Prep as directed. (Can be done up to 3 days ahead).

03.

Make vinaigrette | Mix together red wine vinegar, maple syrup, Dijon mustard, paprika, yogurt, and oil. Season to taste with salt and pepper. (Can be done up to 3 days ahead)

Make

01.

Separate lettuce into lunch containers. Top with chicken, red peppers, corn, and black beans. Separate vinaigrette into small containers (~2 Tbsp / serving). Dress and shake right before enjoying. You can also make mason jar salads by layering dressing, chicken, beans, corn, peppers, and lettuce. Try to keep jar upright and shake right before eating.
